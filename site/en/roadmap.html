Community Roadmap

We build in the open. Check the current status of the v0.1 project and the next steps for solidifying the settlement engine.

v0.1 Alpha [CURRENT]

Infrastructure foundation and definition of microservices architecture bounds.

  • [x] Separation of all 8 core modules into independent repositories.
  • [x] Event bus definition (Kafka/Avro).
  • [x] Atomic Settlement Engine and Ledger proof-of-concept.
  • [x] Initial Sandbox/Docker-Compose structure.

Immediate Next Steps

Current focal point for engineering community contributions.

  • [/] Expanding observability metrics (OpenTelemetry) in numoCore.
  • [/] Improving end-to-end test coverage for the ISO 20022 settlement pipeline.
  • [/] Refining the mTLS infrastructure in the numoTrust module.

Sovereign Evolution (Long-Term Vision)

Covering the complete lifecycle of money and financial data as a Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI).

  • [Phase 2] Open Finance (Governance & Connectivity): Establishing integration standards, trust directories, and governance to enable a secure and open financial ecosystem.
  • [Phase 3] Asset and Contract Registries: Scalable infrastructure for the management of digital assets, receivables, and contracts, ensuring real-time traceability.
  • [Phase 4] Integrated Systemic Supervision: Advanced modules for systemic risk orchestration, global credit monitoring, regulatory compliance, and cross-border capital flow control.

Sovereign technological adoption. Build the Central Bank of the future with us.
