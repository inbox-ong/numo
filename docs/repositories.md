## Repositorios

Catalogo dos repositorios da plataforma Numo.

- numoAdmin: painel administrativo (`https://github.com/inbox-ong/numoAdmin`)
- numoAdmin-next: frontend/admin em Next.js (`https://github.com/inbox-ong/numoAdmin-next`)
- numoCore: regras de negocio e orquestracao (`https://github.com/inbox-ong/numoCore`)
- numoDirectory: catalogo/registro (`https://github.com/inbox-ong/numoDirectory`)
- numoKeys: gestao de chaves e identidades (`https://github.com/inbox-ong/numoKeys`)
- numoLedger: ledger/registro de transacoes (`https://github.com/inbox-ong/numoLedger`)
- numoStream: processamento de eventos/stream (`https://github.com/inbox-ong/numoStream`)
- numoTrust: reputacao/confianca (`https://github.com/inbox-ong/numoTrust`)
